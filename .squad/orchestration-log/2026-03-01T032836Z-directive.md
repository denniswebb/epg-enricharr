## Directive Details
**Topic:** Sports Title Grouping — Regex Pattern Approach

**User Request:**
Use regex patterns (not simple delimiter) for sports title grouping. Patterns are a list; first match wins. Capture groups: 
- Group 1 = sport name/title (e.g., "AFL")
- Group 2 (optional) = match description/subtitle (e.g., "Brisbane vs Sydney")

**Example Pattern:** `^(AFL).*:\s*(.+)$` matches "AFL : AFL : Brisbane vs Sydney" → title="AFL", subtitle="Brisbane vs Sydney"

**Rationale:** Regex is most flexible; handles sponsor prefixes, double-colon titles, and edge cases without manual delimiter logic. Allows multiple patterns with capture groups for both title grouping and subtitle extraction.
